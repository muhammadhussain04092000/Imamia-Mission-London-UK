Learn More About Our Mission

A Place of Worship and Community

Imamia Mission London is a renowned SHIA mosque and charitable organization located in Redbridge, London. We have been proudly serving the SHIA community since the late 1970's, providing a range of religious, educational, and social services. Our mosque at 129 Perryman Farm Road, IG2 7LX, Redbridge, is a hub for spiritual growth, learning, and community support. At Imamia Mission London, we believe in the power of unity, compassion, and knowledge to create a harmonious society.
