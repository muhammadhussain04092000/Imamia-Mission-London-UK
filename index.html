Welcome to Imamia Mission London

Serving the SHIA Community

Imamia Mission London is a registered charity that has been serving the SHIA community in Redbridge, London for several decades. We provide a range of religious and community services, including Islamic studies and Quran classes. Join us and be a part of our mission to promote Islamic teachings and unity among the SHIA community.

About Us

Our Mission and Vision

At Imamia Mission London, our mission is to provide a place of worship and spiritual guidance for the SHIA community in Redbridge. We strive to promote Islamic teachings and values, while fostering a sense of unity, peace, and respect among our community members. Through our various programs and services, we aim to strengthen the faith of our members and contribute to their personal growth.

Our Services


Quran Classes

Our Quran classes are designed to help individuals of all ages learn how to recite and understand the Quran. We have qualified Quran teachers who provide personalised guidance and support to ensure effective learning. Quran classes happen on Mondays and Wednesdays.


Register

Islamic Studies

We offer comprehensive Islamic studies courses for children. Our experienced teachers provide Quranic education, Hadith studies, Akhlaq and Islamic history lessons to help individuals deepen their understanding of Islam.


Register

Charitable Initiatives

As a registered charity, Imamia Mission London actively engages in charitable initiatives to support those in need. We run projects aimed at providing food, clothing, healthcare, and education to underprivileged individuals and families.
